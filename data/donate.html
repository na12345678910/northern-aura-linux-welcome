‌Northern Aura Linux is a community driven GNU/Linux distribution that combines the powerful Ubuntu base with the lightweight LXQt Desktop to provide a user friendly, modern desktop operating system.‌

‌The goal of Northern Aura Linux is to provide a stable operating system that offers a familiar user experience, and usable for both newbies and advanced users alike. We are not part of the official Ubuntu community. We don't get any funding from Canonical (the company that pays for Ubuntu up-keep and development).‌

‌If you believe that our cause is worthy and it is something that needs to go on without any barriers, then please consider contributing to the project.‌
